## Instructions:
1. Mash the banana until it is a paste.
2. Add honey, peanut butter, whey, oats, and almond milk to the banana and whisk all the ingredients. Set aside.
3. Start a pot of boiling water then put a bowl on top and melt the chocolate.
4. Once choolate is mleted, stir into the rest of the ingredients.
5. Get some wax paper, lay it in a container with edges, and pour the mix into the container.
6. Store in freezer and let it freeze overnight.
7. Once frozen cut it into 8 equal pieces.
