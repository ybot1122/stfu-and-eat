## Instructions:
1. Mash the banana until it is a paste.

<ExpandableImage
  src="/recipes/whey-oats-chocolate-bar/mashed-banana.jpg"
  alt="Mashed banana in a bowl" />
2. Add honey, peanut butter, whey, oats, and almond milk to the banana and whisk all the ingredients. Set aside.

<ExpandableImage
  src="/recipes/whey-oats-chocolate-bar/combine-ingredients.jpg"
  alt="Combining ingredients" />
3. Start a pot of boiling water then put a bowl on top and melt the chocolate.

<ExpandableImage
  src="/recipes/whey-oats-chocolate-bar/melting-chocolate.jpg"
  alt="Melting the chocolate" />
4. Once choolate is mleted, stir into the rest of the ingredients.

5. Get some wax paper, lay it in a container with edges, and pour the mix into the container.

<ExpandableImage
  src="/recipes/whey-oats-chocolate-bar/in-the-pan.jpg"
  alt="Spreading the batter" />

6. Use a spatula to spread chocolate over the top.

<ExpandableImage
  src="/recipes/whey-oats-chocolate-bar/in-the-freezer.jpg"
  alt="Preparing to store in freezer" />
7. Store in freezer and let it freeze overnight.

8. Once frozen cut it into 8 equal pieces.

<ExpandableImage
  src="/recipes/whey-oats-chocolate-bar/finished.jpg"
  alt="Preparing to store in freezer" />
